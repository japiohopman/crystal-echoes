Game Concept

Core Story

Ancient crystals contain the essence of magic and memory. As industrial technology harnesses crystal energy for Magitek machinery, the balance between nature and technology is disrupted.

World Setting

A diverse world spanning lush forests, imposing mountains, steampunk cities, and ancient ruins. Each region tells part of the larger story through environmental storytelling.

Characters

Multiple playable characters with unique backgrounds and motivations. Personal journeys intersect with the larger conflict between preservation and progress.

Key Features

Active Time Battle System

Strategic combat with timing elements

Crystal Resonance Magic

Customizable abilities through crystal attunement

Expansive World

Diverse regions with unique environments

Branching Storylines

Player choices influence character development

Rich Side Content

Quests and secrets reward exploration

16-bit Pixel Art

Classic aesthetic with modern polish
